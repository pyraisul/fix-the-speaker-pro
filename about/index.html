About FixMySpeaker

We got tired of the rice-bag method. So we built something that actually works — and made it free for everyone.

How We Got Started

FixMySpeaker started the way most tools do — with annoyance. A phone fell in the sink. The speaker sounded terrible. The internet said "put it in rice and wait 48 hours." That felt ridiculous.

We knew sound frequencies could vibrate moisture out of speakers (Apple does it with the Watch). So we built a simple browser tool to do the same thing. No app needed, no charge, no signup.

That side project turned into something real. Over 500,000 people have used FixMySpeaker to rescue their devices, and we're still making it better.

The Tech Behind It

FixMySpeaker combines browser-native audio tools with acoustic science to deliver real results:

🔊 Web Audio API

We use the Web Audio API to generate tones with sub-hertz precision. This lets us dial in the exact frequency needed for water ejection or dust removal — all without requiring any app or plugin.

🌊 Water Eject Technology

The 165Hz tone is calibrated to make the speaker membrane oscillate at just the right speed to push water droplets outward through the grille. It’s the same frequency range used in Apple Watch’s built-in water eject — proven and safe.

💨 Multi-Frequency Dust Removal

Dust particles vary in size, so a single frequency won’t cut it. We cycle through 200Hz to 6400Hz to shake loose everything from fine lint to coarse grit — covering the full spectrum of common speaker debris.

📳 Vibration Enhancement

When sound alone doesn’t do the trick, we combine audio with the device’s Vibration API. The physical shaking adds mechanical force that helps dislodge stubborn blockages.

🎚️ Stereo Control

Stereo panning lets us route the cleaning signal to just the left speaker, just the right, or both at once. This means precise, targeted cleaning without wasting effort on a speaker that’s fine.

⚡ Real-Time Processing

Everything happens on your device, in your browser. No server uploads, no processing delays, no privacy worries. Just instant, local speaker cleaning.
